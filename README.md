## About Me

Backend-focused software engineer with 5+ years of experience designing and building **secure, cloud-native, distributed systems** in enterprise environments.

My professional background spans **insurance, energy, and integration-heavy digital platforms**, where backend systems demand not only scalability, but also **reliability, observability, maintainability, and strong security boundaries**.

I specialize in building backend services with **.NET, Azure, microservices, asynchronous messaging, and modern architecture patterns**, with growing hands-on focus on **Go-based concurrent systems** and **AI-assisted engineering workflows**.
